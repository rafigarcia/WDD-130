Overview

Purpose:

The purpose of Rafaela Advertising's personal branding website is to establish a strong online presence and showcase Rafaela Advertising's expertise, passions, and professional background. By providing valuable content, insights, and resources, the website aims to position Rafaela Garcia as a thought leader in Advertising Market. Through this platform, Rafaela Advertising's will share insights, experiences, and industry trends, offering a unique perspective on the ad. The website will serve as a hub for Rafaela Advertising's professional achievements, portfolio, and accomplishments, providing visitors with a comprehensive view of Rafaela Advertising's skills, capabilities, and achievements.

Audience

As a potential employer, I want recruiters, hiring managers, and companies to visit my personal branding website to learn more about my skills, experience, and achievements. I aim to create a strong impression and demonstrate why I am a valuable candidate for employment opportunities or freelance projects. For potential clients or customers, I want my website to target individuals who are seeking services or consulting in my area of expertise. I aim to showcase my skills and experience, clearly communicate the value I provide, and highlight how I can help address their needs or goals. By sharing case studies, testimonials, or examples of successful projects, I aim to build trust and credibility with potential clients or customers.
